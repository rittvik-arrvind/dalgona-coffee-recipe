Dalgona Coffee Recipe

This recipe is pretty famous in the internet. This South Korean recipe has its own seperate fan base as a lockdown activity. Having partnered with Whatsapp status,this reached every nook and corner of the world.You might have the ingredients in the cup board.

Ingredients:

  • Instant coffee powder or expresso granuels
  • Hot water
  • Sugar
  • Milk

Dalgona Making Process

Step 1: Add coffee powder to hot water
Add 2 table spoons(tbsp) of coffee powderand 3 table spoons(tbsp) of sugar to 1 cup of hot water. The water must be boiling hot.
Step2: Whisk,Whisk,Whisk until the coffee mixture becomes fluffy.
Using a hand held mixer whisk the mixture till it becomes creamy. It will be light brown in colour. Dont be lazy to whisk.
Step 3:Serve with hot/cold milk.
Serve with hot/cold milk. A straw and spoon would be mandate. Now stir,stir,stir to enjoy this lockdown recipe!!!
